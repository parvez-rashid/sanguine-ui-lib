Hamburger

A hamburger menu is an icon used on a website and in apps that, when clicked or tapped, opens a side menu or navigation drawer. It’s called a “hamburger menu” because it takes the form of the famous sandwich, i.e., the Hamburger. Include class hamburger to use this component.
