Alerts

Alerts are used to draw the user's attention to information which is not a prt of the normal view, without affecting the user's flow.

Usage of alerts

Alerts are available in 4 types - primary, success, warning, and danger. Simply use class alert alongwith class of type of alert which are alert-primary, alert-secondary, alert-warning, or alert-danger.

  • Primary alert is used to display general information to the user.
  • Success alert is used to display total success or completion of any action to the user.
  • Warning alert is used to display partial success or information that is important for the user to check.
  • Danger alert is used to display total failure or information that is critical for the user to check.
